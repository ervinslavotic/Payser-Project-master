The Payser Project

The Payser project is a secure, cloud based storage solution for your private documents. Our goal is to make life easier for the user, in regards to the task of organising and managing important documents.

Features - What makes us stand out

  • Mail Search - We can filter your Gmail account and add filtered documents to our storage area.
  • Indexed Storage - All private documents are indexed for quick searches.
  • Graph system - Our system evaluates payslips and sets up a detailed graph depicting earnings, tax paid, etc.

The Employer

The Payser Project enables you, as an employer, to automate and manage the job of issuing paychecks and payslips to your employees.

  • - Keep track of employee records with our secure database.
  • - Use our automated service to send payslips to you employees.
